Summary of Key Points

At Brotherz Gaming, we prioritize transparency regarding data handling in PixL:

  • We do not collect or store your personal information directly. Third-party services (Google Analytics, Firebase, Unity Ads, Patreon) may collect data for analytics, ads, and membership verification.
  • Our role is minimal. We only perform a temporary Patreon API check to confirm ad-free membership status, without storing this data.
  • Privacy rights. Direct data-related requests to these third-party services, as we hold no personal data.

1. What Information Is Collected?

We do not collect or store personal information directly. However, third-party services in PixL may collect:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Usage and device data.

  • Unity Ads: IP address, Android Advertising ID for ads.
  • Patreon: Membership status via API (not stored by us).

Examples: Device model, OS version, IP address, app interactions.

Note: We don’t control these practices. See their policies in Section 4.

We do not process personal information ourselves. Third parties process data for:

Our only action is a temporary Patreon API check, with no storage or further processing.
